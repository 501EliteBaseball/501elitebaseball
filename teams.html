Season Blueprint

How the Year Is Built

FallLeague + 4 tournaments

A competitive foundation with enough reps to grow without burning kids out.

Spring8–10 tournaments

The main competition season with intentional practice structure and communication.

TravelMax 4 overnight tournaments

Family time matters. Travel is planned, communicated, and purposeful.

Development

More Than a Lineup

At 8U, the goal is not to make kids feel like professionals. The goal is to help them love the game, learn how to compete, and build habits that last.

01Confidence

Players learn through reps, encouragement, and clear expectations.

02Skill Growth

Throwing, catching, fielding, hitting, baserunning, and baseball IQ.

03Character

Effort, attitude, accountability, respect, and being a good teammate.
