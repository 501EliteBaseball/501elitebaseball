A Letter From Our Founders

Why We Started 501 Elite

To our 501 Elite families,

We started 501 Elite because we believe youth baseball can be competitive, organized, and meaningful without losing sight of the kids. Winning matters, and development matters, but the greater purpose is helping young players learn how to work, respond to adversity, respect the game, and carry themselves with confidence.

At 8U, every practice and game is an opportunity to build habits that reach far beyond baseball. We want our players to feel challenged, supported, and proud of the team they represent. We also want families to feel informed, respected, and connected to what we are creating together.

Thank you for trusting us with your players and for helping establish the standard that will define this program for years to come.

Adam Thomas & Chase GrimmettCo-Founders & Executive Directors
