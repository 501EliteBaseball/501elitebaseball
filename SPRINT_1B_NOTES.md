# Sprint 1B — Refine, Don't Reinvent

Focus: Correct the Sprint 1 art direction drift and return the site to a restrained premium baseball identity.

## Changes
- Simplified the global header so it frames the page instead of competing with the hero.
- Removed the heavy branded/floating header treatment and restored a cleaner logo-left/menu-right system.
- Rebuilt homepage player cards as premium shield-led placeholders instead of oversized “501” graphics.
- Removed the standalone “501” treatment from the homepage CTA line.
- Rebuilt sponsor tier card styling with restrained metallic finishes and the official shield mark.
- Expanded the About page with a fuller story, mission, differentiators, leadership section, and future-facing CTA.

## Design Direction
- Shield becomes the signature brand element.
- No standalone “501” graphic treatment.
- More restraint, better spacing, and fewer decorative effects.
